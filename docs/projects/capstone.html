Overview

The senior capstone design project represents the culmination of my undergraduate electrical engineering education. This multi-semester project encompassed the full engineering design lifecycle: requirements analysis, conceptual design, detailed design, prototyping, testing, and final design review.

Design Process

  • Requirements Analysis: Defined project scope, constraints, and success criteria through stakeholder engagement and technical feasibility assessment.
  • Systems Engineering: Applied structured systems engineering methodology to decompose the problem, allocate requirements to subsystems, and manage interfaces.
  • Design Reviews: Conducted formal design reviews including midterm progress reports and a comprehensive final design review presentation.
  • Technical Documentation: Produced detailed engineering reports documenting design decisions, analysis results, test data, and lessons learned.
